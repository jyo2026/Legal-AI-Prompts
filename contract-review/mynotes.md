Below are the best practices for contract review

Party names
Check party names and basic identification markers like CIN, address, age if any
Check if there are equal number of signatories mentioned in the end of the contract

Recital clause
Check the recital clause for brief background
MAIN
Check clauses - tenure, payment terms, scope of work, obligations
Check clauses - confidentiality clause, data protection
Indemnity and limitation of liability
Check - Jurisdiction clause and governing law
Check - residuary clauses

Final version
Ensure to procure stamp paper and get signatures of each signatory party
